## Making Routines Stick

### The Review Problem

You know you should do reviews. You don't do them consistently. This is normal.

**Strategies that help:**

1. **Same time, same place** â€” Consistency builds habit
2. **Calendar block** â€” It's an appointment with yourself
3. **Body doubling** â€” Do it with someone nearby (even if they're doing their own thing)
4. **Shorter is better** â€” 20-minute review > skipped 60-minute review
5. **Reward** â€” Coffee, snack, or something pleasant during/after

### When You Miss a Routine

**Morning planning skipped?**
- Flag one task. Just one. Do that.
- Or open Quick Wins and do anything.

**Shutdown skipped?**
- Tomorrow's morning planning will catch it.
- Don't process inbox tonight â€” it's tomorrow's problem.

**Weekly review skipped?**
- Don't double up next week.
- Do a quick 15-minute "emergency review" instead.
- See [RECIPES.md â†’ Catching Up](RECIPES.md#catching-up-after-missing-reviews)
